About Star Aviation India

STAR AVIATION INDIA is proprietorship Company incorporated in 2011 at New Delhi, India. Since then, the company is engaged in supply of aviation equipment for all type of Russian/CIS made Aircraft and Helicopters.

We would be pleased to inform you that Star Aviation India”SAI” is also authorized by OEM to undertake different types of repair / Overhaul of major assemblies such as gear boxes, tail rotor and tail rotor blades etc.

Star Aviation India “SAI” has long term agreement with Russian OEMs/Plants for supply. repair and overhaul of aviation equipments.

Star Aviation India “SAI” has mutual agreement with Russian Aviation Company Ltd for support and supply of vide range of consumable and standardized aviation items for all type of Russian aircrafts and Helicopters, Including hoses, pipes, valves, blocks and ground equipment.

We are also engaged in design, Development, Supply & Repair of COMPONENTS OF RUSSIAN ORIGIN AIRCRAFT/HELICOPTER, OTHER DEFENCE SYSTEM, GHE/GSE, RADAR & COMMUNICATION SYSTEM, HEAVY MACHINERY AND TOOLS & TESTER. We also provide LOGISTICS SUPPORT TO THE FOREIGN OEM, REPRESENTATION OF FOREIGN OEM IN INDIA, CHARTER SERVICES: LEASE/PURCHASE OF HELICOPTERS & AIRCRAFTS & MARKETING RESEARCH REPORTS, BUSINESS REPORTS FOR INDIA, INDIAN SUBCONTINENT COUNTRIES NEPAL, BANGLADESH, SRILANKA, VIETNAM & SOUTH EAST ASIAN COUNTRIES.
